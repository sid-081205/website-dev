Why this topic

As humanity stands on the brink of interplanetary exploration, understanding planetary habitability has never been more critical. The Star Wars universe presents a unique laboratory of diverse worlds - from the desert plains of Tatooine to the forest moon of Endor - each with distinct environmental characteristics that could inform our real-world approach to planetary colonization.

Furthermore, as private space companies advance toward Mars colonization and beyond, having robust planetary assessment tools becomes essential for mission planning and human survival. The fictional worlds of Star Wars, with their detailed ecosystems and civilizations, provide rich data sets for testing habitability models that could one day guide humanity's expansion among the stars.
